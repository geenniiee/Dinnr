Tacos

25 minutes

7 ingredients

Ingredients
• Taco Shells
• Tortillas
• Onions
• Shredded Cheddar Cheese
• Lettuce
• Tomatoes
• Taco Shells
• Taco Seasoning

Directions

1. Heat taco shells and tortillas as directed on each package.
2. Chop and shred lettuce and tomatoes, then set each aside in a bowl.
3. Sautee onions on medium-high until translucent, then add ground beef and the taco seasoning. Cook until done, it should take about 8-10 minutes.
4. Set taco meat in a bowl and starting arranging the prepped ingredients. Customize and enjoy tacos!
